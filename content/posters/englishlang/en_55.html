WORD BUILDING

Prefixes and Suffixes (Словообразование)

NOUNS (Существительные)

-ION: Action (Action)
-MENT: Improve (Improvement)
-NESS: Happy (Happiness)
-ITY: Active (Activity)
-ER/OR: Teach (Teacher)

ADJECTIVES (Прилагательные)

-FUL: Help (Helpful)
-LESS: Hope (Hopeless)
-AL: Culture (Cultural)
-ABLE: Comfort (Comfortable)
-OUS: Danger (Dangerous)

VERBS (Глаголы)

-IZE: Modern (Modernize)
-EN: Strength (Strengthen)
-IFY: Simple (Simplify)

ADVERBS (Наречия)

-LY: Quick (Quickly)
-WARD: Home (Homeward)

PREFIXES (Префиксы)

UN-: Happy (UnHappy) - Negative
RE-: Write (ReWrite) - Again
PRE-: View (PreView) - Before
MIS-: Understand (MisUnderstand) - Wrong
DIS-: Like (DisLike) - Opposite
