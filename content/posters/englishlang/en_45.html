üìñ What is Future Continuous?

The Future Continuous (also called Future Progressive) is used to describe an action that will be in progress at a specific moment in the future. It shows that an action will be ongoing when something else happens or at a particular time.

üéØ When to Use Future Continuous

Usage Example
1. Action in progress at a specific future time At 3 o'clock, I will be studying.
2. Two actions: one (FC) when another (FS) happens When you arrive, I will be cooking dinner.
3. Duration: how long an action will take I will be working for 8 hours tomorrow.
4. Natural consequence/result Next summer, she will be studying for exams.
5. Assumption about the future He will probably be sleeping when you call.

üìù Common Patterns
