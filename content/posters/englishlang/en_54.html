PHRASAL VERBS – PART 2

Advanced Level (Grade 10–11)

GIVE UP
Stop doing something (Бросить)
Don't give up on your dreams.
LOOK AFTER
Take care of (Заботиться)
She looks after her little sister.
CARRY ON
Continue (Продолжать)
Please carry on reading.
TURN UP
Arrive / Appear (Появиться)
He finally turned up at the party.
SET UP
Create / Organize (Организовать)
They set up a new company.
RUN OUT OF
Have no more (Заканчиваться)
We ran out of time.
