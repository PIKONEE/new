üìñ What is Future Simple?

The Future Simple (also called Simple Future or Future Indefinite) is used to talk about actions or events that will happen in the future. It expresses predictions, decisions, promises, offers, and planned actions.

‚ùå Negative:

‚ùì Question:

üéØ When to Use Future Simple

Usage Example
1. Predictions (based on opinion) I think it will rain tomorrow.
2. Spontaneous decisions (right now) "The phone is ringing!" "I will answer it."
3. Promises and offers I will help you with your homework.
4. Facts about the future (won't change) The sun will rise tomorrow at 6 AM.
5. Threats or warnings If you don't study, you will fail the test.
6. General statements He will be 25 years old next year.
